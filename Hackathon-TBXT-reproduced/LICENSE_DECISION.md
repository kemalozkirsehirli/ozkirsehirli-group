# License Decision

This public release uses a custom source-available, non-commercial research license:

```text
TBXT Research Source License v1.1
```

It is **not** an OSI-approved open-source license.

## Why not MIT / Apache / BSD?

This repository contains hackathon research artifacts, CADD/AI workflows, generated candidates, reports, small result summaries, and preserved collaborative contributions. A broad permissive license could unintentionally authorize commercial reuse, redistribution, or downstream drug-discovery use beyond the intended research release.

The custom license is more cautious: it allows public inspection, non-commercial research review, reproducibility, education, citation, and local experimentation while restricting commercial use, false attribution, false maintainership claims, patent-sensitive use, and use of third-party materials beyond their own terms.

## Important boundary

The license applies only to material for which the applicable rights holders have authority to license. It does not adjudicate ownership disputes and does not grant rights to third-party datasets, models, compound catalogs, APIs, hosted bundles, trademarks, or institutional materials.

## Where governance lives

- License terms: `LICENSE`
- Attribution: `AUTHORS.md`, `CONTRIBUTORS.md`, `NOTICE.md`
- Current GitHub repository maintainership: `MAINTAINERS.md`, `GOVERNANCE.md`
- Third-party limits: `THIRD_PARTY_AND_DATA_NOTICE.md`
- Safety/risk-reduction changes: `PUBLIC_RELEASE_SAFETY_NOTES.md`
