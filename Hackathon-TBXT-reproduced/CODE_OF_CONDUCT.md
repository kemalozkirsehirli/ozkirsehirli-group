# Code of Conduct

This repository is maintained for serious scientific and engineering work. Contributors are expected to act professionally, document claims, preserve attribution, and avoid conduct that undermines research integrity or team safety.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful in issues, pull requests, reviews, and project communications.
- Attribute work accurately.
- Do not claim leadership, ownership, maintainership, or endorsement you do not have.
- Keep scientific claims tied to data, scripts, notebooks, reports, or cited sources.
- Raise disputes with evidence and a clear proposed correction.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, personal attacks, or retaliation.
- Misrepresenting another person's work as your own.
- Misrepresenting repository governance or maintainer authority.
- Removing attribution without evidence and maintainer approval.
- Publishing private credentials, private datasets, personal contact details, or access tokens.

## Enforcement

The repository maintainer may edit issues, close discussions, reject pull requests, revoke access, or block users when needed to protect research integrity, contributor safety, or repository governance.

## Reporting

Report concerns to the current maintainer listed in `MAINTAINERS.md`. Include specific links, files, screenshots, commits, or other records whenever possible.
